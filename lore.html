Lore

Planet Earth is deteriorating. It’s being destroyed by Wars, Pollution, and Greed. As a way to escape, NOVA SOLUTIONS creates the StarCruiser, a huge Spacecraft that is filled with activities for Recreational uses. There’s sports, hangouts, a utopia in the stars. Humans need to upload their consciousness into the NOVA-1, a Multipurpose Athletic Robot, the new you! But one day, Earth is destroyed in a catastrophic event.. (or atleast that’s what they want us to think). So the Robots are left on their own - waiting for a Signal or Message from NOVA SOLUTIONS.
